World Famous Lasagna

A delicious spin on everyone's favorite comfort meal! With layer after layer of saucy, cheesiness, you will be salvating for the next night with this on the table!

When I think of lasagna I imagine everyone sitting around the table, sharing stories of their day. A crisp ceaser salad passing around and the smell of garlic toast wafting through the air. It was always the "special" meal in the house. The one meal that mom only made every once in a while and we all begged for it on the in-between.

Today I'm sharing a version I've been perfecting over the years. It's still the same lasagna that everyone knows and loves. I just made a couple changes that boost, this already amazing entree, into the next level.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Ground Beef (Faux beef)
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Parsley
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Brown beef
  2. Add sauce to pan with beef
  3. Cook Noodles
  4. While noodles cook, mix pasrley, mozzarella, seasonings and ricotta in a bowl.
  5. Layer meat sauce , noodles and cheese mix into a cassarole dish.
  6. Cook at 350 farenheit for 30 minutes.
  7. Let it set for 10 minutes once out of oven then ENJOY!
